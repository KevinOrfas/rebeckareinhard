And all your friends are moving to London, while the cherry trees are still in blossom

Jens Lekman

I love this line from Jens Lekman’s Julie off Oh You’re So Silent Jens, a lyrically brilliant album that I’ve listened to throughout my young adulthood, first in the Stockholm suburb I grew up in, and then while trying to make sense of the world in Paris. All of my friends did move to London at one point, when we were all fed up with the French (decadent lifestyle). I, however, did a 4-year-detour to Gothenburg before making it to this overpriced, chaotic, incomprehensible place. Most of my friends had moved on or moved home, but the cherry trees were in full bloom and I fell in love with the city.

About

An enigmatic, London based singer/songwriter, Rebecka Reinhard is currently recording her debut EP for release in early 2017.

2016 has seen Rebecka showcase her unrelenting mystique throughout London’s vibrant live music scene, adorned solely with her prized Ibanez. And as her solo reputation blossoms, so has her sound. Taking influence from its indie folk-pop roots and blending in elements of country, Americana and grunge, Rebecka’s enchanting vocals now come complimented by full back-up band ensemble.

With this newly formed band and a debut EP set for release next year, Rebecka Reinhard’s distinctive charm is set to be a massive fixture of 2017. But don’t just take our word for it, get immersed in her world for yourselves and check out all the music below…
